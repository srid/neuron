Clusters

Your Zettelkasten may have two or more clusters, not connected to one another. Impulse will display these clusters, with each cluster’s Folgezettel Heterarchy rendered as a forest, whose roots (aka “mother zettels”) could be considered as a portal zettel into that sub-Zettelkasten.
